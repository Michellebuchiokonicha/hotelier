Eiffel Tower Summit Direct Access by Elevator

(35 Reviews) 1 Day Paris, france

About tour

Join a tour of Paris to explore some of its iconic landmarks, rich history, and diverse culture. We'll begin at the Eiffel Tower to marvel at the panoramic views of the city. Then, we'll visit the Notre Dame Cathedral, stroll down the Champs-Élysées, and pay our respects at the Arc de Triomphe. At the Louvre Museum, we'll admire the incredible art pieces, including the famous Mona Lisa. And, we'll indulge in Paris's famous cuisine. Finally, we'll take a romantic boat ride down the Seine River to see the city's beauty from a new perspective.

Itinerary

Eiffel Tower, Champs-Elysées, Louvre Museum, Place de la Concorde

Visit the Eiffel Tower and take a tour to the top of the tower for panoramic views of Paris. Explore the Champs-Elysées avenue, known for its high-end fashion boutiques, cafes and cinemas. Stroll through the Jardin des Tuileries, a beautiful park located between the Louvre Museum and the Place de la Concorde.

Winged Victory of Samothrace, Venus de Milo, Sainte Chapelle

Spend the day at the Louvre Museum, home to famous works of art such as the Mona Lisa, the Winged Victory of Samothrace, and the Venus de Milo. Visit the nearby Sainte Chapelle, a Gothic chapel known for its stained glass windows. Take a stroll along the Seine River.

Palace of Versailles, Hall of Mirrors

Take a day trip to the Palace of Versailles, a stunning royal chateau and UNESCO World Heritage site located just outside of Paris. Explore the vast gardens, the Hall of Mirrors, and the ornate apartments of the kings and queens of France.

Included

  • Guide: Small-Group Tour
  • Meals: Breakfast, lunch, dinner every day
  • Additional Services: Seine River Cruise, Bordeaux City Pass
  • Transport: High-speed train from Paris to Bordeaux
  • Flights: International flights are not included
  • Insurance: Travel insurance is not included
  • Transfers: Service not included
